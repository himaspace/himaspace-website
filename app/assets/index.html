Himalaya Makerspace
Arcos de Valdevez

We are creating a Makerspace. An open community, where people from all ages and backgrounds feel welcome to learn about and play with technology. A space where innovation happens, naturally and spontaneously. Where knowledge and skills are shared. A community envisioning a future, together.

Amongst some of the technologies we hope to teach, share, play and create with are:

  • Automation with Arduino and Raspberry Pi
  • 3D printing and autofabrication
  • Programming and Open Source collaboration
  • Linux and Free and Open Source software (FOSS)
  • Recycling and refurbishing of outdated computers and hardware
  • Online self-defense through security and cryptography
